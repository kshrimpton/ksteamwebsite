About

Compiling a list of must-see sights is arguably the most exciting part of planning a holiday. But how many of us compile a list of must-eat foods?

That's no problem when you can flick through the menu at your destination and pick at will, but what if the menu is written in a language you don’t understand, or worse, a language you can’t even read . . .

Foodies in Asia brings you iconic, must-eat dishes from across East Asia, familiarising you with these amazing cuisines and ensuring you don’t miss out on mind-blowing deliciousness. With Foodies in Asia, get ready to eat like a local.
